About Itinera

Itinera is a web-based app designed to simplify your trips by keeping your itinerary, packing list, and travel expenses all in one place. No more juggling multiple apps or messy notes—plan your trips efficiently and stress-free!

How to Get Started

  1. Go to the Itinerary page to add your trip activities.
  2. Go to the Packing List page to add items you need to bring.
  3. Track your expenses on the Expense Tracker page to keep your budget in check.
  4. Use the navigation links below to move between pages anytime.
  5. Click the “Delete” buttons next to items to remove anything you no longer need.
